Nephrology

At Nova Health, our nephrology department is dedicated to providing high-quality care for patients with kidney diseases and disorders.

Conditions We Treat

  • Chronic kidney disease (CKD)
  • Acute kidney injury (AKI)
  • Kidney stones
  • Kidney cancer
  • End-stage renal disease (ESRD)

Treatments We Offer

  • Dialysis
  • Kidney transplantation
  • Medications
  • Lifestyle modifications
  • Kidney biopsy
