Urology

At Nova Health, our urology department is dedicated to providing high-quality care for patients with urinary tract disorders.

  • Overactive bladder
  • Kidney stones

Treatments and Services

  • Prostate biopsy
  • Urinary tract infection (UTI) treatment
  • Bladder cancer treatment
  • Kidney stone removal
