Online Lab Reports

At Nova Health, we understand the importance of timely access to your lab results. Our online lab report system allows you to view and manage your test results securely and conveniently from any location.

How to Access Your Lab Reports Online

  1. Step 1: Log In to Your Patient Portal

    Visit our patient portal.

    Enter your username and password to log in. If you do not have an account, you can easily create one by following the registration process.

  2. Step 2: Navigate to Lab Reports

    Once logged in, go to the “Lab Reports” section in your dashboard.

    Here, you will see a list of all your recent lab tests and their statuses.

  3. Step 3: View Your Results

    Click on the test you wish to view.

    Your lab report will be displayed, including detailed results and any notes from your healthcare provider.

  4. Step 4: Download or Print Your Report

    You can download a PDF version of your lab report for your records.

    There is also an option to print the report directly from the portal.

Benefits of Online Lab Reports

  • Immediate Access: View your lab results as soon as they are available.
  • Secure and Confidential: Your health information is protected with advanced security measures.
  • Convenience: Access your reports from your computer, tablet, or smartphone.
  • Environmentally Friendly: Reduce paper usage by viewing and downloading reports electronically.
