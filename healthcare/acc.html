Accreditation

At Nova Health, we are committed to providing high-quality care to our patients. We are proud to have received accreditation from several reputable organizations, recognizing our commitment to excellence in patient care and safety.

Accrediting Organizations

  • The Joint Commission

    We have been accredited by The Joint Commission, a non-profit organization that sets standards for healthcare quality and safety.

  • American College of Surgeons

    We have been accredited by the American College of Surgeons, a professional organization that sets standards for surgical care.

  • College of American Pathologists

    We have been accredited by the College of American Pathologists, a professional organization that sets standards for laboratory testing and quality.

What Accreditation Means

Accreditation is a voluntary process that demonstrates our commitment to providing high-quality care to our patients. It means that we have met rigorous standards for patient safety, quality of care, and operational efficiency.

Accreditation benefits our patients in several ways, including:

  • Improved patient outcomes
  • Enhanced patient safety
  • Better communication and coordination of care
  • Increased accountability and transparency
